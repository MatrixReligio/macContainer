# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other protected characteristic.

Constructive technical disagreement is welcome. Personal attacks,
discrimination, intimidation, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption,
doxxing, and publishing another person's private information are not.

## Enforcement

Community leaders may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily
or permanently restrict participation when conduct is inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful. Enforcement decisions should be
proportionate, documented privately, and avoid retaliation.

Report conduct concerns privately to contact@matrixreligio.com. Reports are
handled with discretion; conflicts of interest are reassigned when possible.
This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project.

This code is informed by Contributor Covenant 2.1 and its community-impact
guidelines.
