# Governance

MacContainer is maintained by MatrixReligio LLC using a maintainer-led,
evidence-first model. Maintainers set product direction, review contributions,
manage releases, and protect the security and compatibility boundaries.

## Decisions

Routine changes are accepted through reviewed pull requests. Significant
changes to architecture, privileges, privacy, compatibility policy, or public
contracts require a written proposal with alternatives, risks, tests, and a
recorded maintainer decision. Consensus is preferred; the project lead makes
the final decision when consensus cannot be reached.

Decisions must be based on current code, pinned upstream evidence, and observed
test results. Commercial affiliation does not bypass review. Maintainers must
disclose relevant conflicts of interest and recuse themselves when practical.

## Roles

- Contributors submit issues, code, design, documentation, and verification.
- Reviewers may approve within areas where they have demonstrated expertise.
- Maintainers merge changes, triage security reports, and operate releases.
- The project lead appoints or removes maintainers based on sustained,
  trustworthy participation.

Repository access follows least privilege and may be revoked after inactivity
or a security concern. Governance questions may be sent to
contact@matrixreligio.com. Conduct matters follow the
[Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
